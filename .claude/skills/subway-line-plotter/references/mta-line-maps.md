# MTA Official Line Maps & Station Orders

Source: [mta.info/maps/subway-line-maps](https://www.mta.info/maps/subway-line-maps/)

These are the **authoritative station orders** per line. When implementing or validating a line, cross-reference against this list. Station names here reflect the official MTA platform name for each line's stop.

## Important: Complex vs. Platform Naming

The same physical complex can have **different station names** depending on which line/platform you're on:

| Complex Area | A/C/E Platform | R/W Platform | 1/2/3 Platform | E Terminal |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| WTC / Chambers / Cortlandt | **Chambers St** | **Cortlandt St** | **Chambers St** (1), **Park Place** (2/3) | **World Trade Center** |
| 42nd St Midtown | **42 St/Port Authority** (A/C/E) | **Times Sq-42 St** (N/Q/R/W) | **Times Sq-42 St** (1/2/3) | **42 St/Port Authority** |
| 14th St 8th Ave | **14 St** (A/C/E/L) | **14 St-Union Sq** (N/Q/R/W) | **14 St** (1/2/3) | **14 St** |

Always use the station name that matches the **specific line** being implemented.
